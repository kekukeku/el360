One-Stop Corporate ESG Sustainability Self-Assessment Platform

Synesgy is a global ESG self-assessment and certification platform developed by CRIF that helps companies comprehensively understand their sustainability performance. Companies simply complete an online questionnaire covering supply chain and internal process topics to quickly complete a self-diagnosis spanning management, environmental, social, governance, and other dimensions. The system instantly generates ESG scores and visual analytics reports, and awards Synesgy certification aligned with international standards such as the SDGs, GRI, and ESRS. Companies can integrate certificates on their websites or social media to demonstrate sustainability commitment to customers and investors.

What is Synesgy?

The Synesgy platform, launched by global credit scoring agency CRIF, is a digital tool that enables companies and suppliers to self-assess sustainability performance. It supports more than 25 languages across multiple countries and regions. The platform provides assessment questionnaires covering five sustainability dimensions: "Management & Governance, Environment, Social, and Industry-Specific Topics," with a simplified version designed for SMEs with fewer than 50 employees. Through Synesgy, companies can conduct self-assessments, collect and manage ESG data for themselves and their suppliers, identify potential risks, and plan improvement pathways.

Why Choose Synesgy?

The Synesgy platform offers multiple advantages that make it a preferred choice for companies driving sustainable development. First, backed by CRIF's extensive rating experience and rigorous methodology, questionnaire design references international standards including GRI, the UN Global Compact, and the Carbon Disclosure Project. In 2024, it received official GRI certification, ensuring assessment results have credibility and international comparability. Second, Synesgy supports multilingual and cross-border applications, currently deployed in over 60 countries. It provides intuitive data dashboards and industry benchmark analysis, enabling real-time monitoring of company and supply chain ESG performance and rapid identification of operational or reputational risks. Finally, upon completing the questionnaire, companies receive Synesgy certification certificates aligned with international standards, which can be published on websites or social channels to enhance external trust and brand image. In summary, the Synesgy platform not only helps companies meet the latest compliance requirements but also provides comprehensive, reliable sustainability data insights, strengthening corporate competitiveness and transparency.

How It Works: Assessment Process & Report Contents

The Synesgy usage process is simple and intuitive:

  1. Register and Input Data: Company managers create an account on the platform, input basic operational information and organizational structure, and complete relevant data indicators.
  2. Complete the ESG Questionnaire: Answer tailored questionnaires based on five dimensions (management, environment, social, governance, industry). The system automatically provides standard or simplified versions based on company size.
  3. Receive Report and Certificate: The system automatically generates ESG scores, charts of key indicators, and analytical reports, and awards Synesgy certificates aligned with international standards. Companies can publish certificates on official websites or social media to demonstrate sustainability commitment through concrete action.
    Through these reports, managers can clearly understand company and supply chain strengths and weaknesses, and develop sustainability strategies based on system-provided improvement recommendations.

Target Users & Application Scenarios

Synesgy serves a broad range of users: from multinational corporations to small and medium suppliers. Supply chain management is one of its core functions: large enterprises in manufacturing and retail can use the platform to conduct ESG assessments of upstream and downstream suppliers, identify potential risks, and strengthen compliance; suppliers can conduct self-assessments and receive further improvement recommendations. Additionally, financial institutions such as banks and insurers can use Synesgy as a customer sustainability risk scoring tool; industry associations can use the platform to help member companies improve sustainability standards. In short, any organization focused on corporate social responsibility and sustainable transformation can benefit from the Synesgy platform.

Synesgy Results & Value

To date, Synesgy has been deployed in over 90 countries, serving hundreds of thousands of companies and thousands of financial institutions. Through the platform, companies can quantify ESG performance and obtain clear ESG scores and improvement pathways. Synesgy certificates obtained after completing assessments align with international standards such as GRI and the SDGs; companies can display certificates on websites or social platforms to demonstrate commitment and achievements on sustainability issues to customers and investors. This data and certification not only enhance brand reputation but also help companies meet international compliance requirements and strengthen market competitiveness. In summary, Synesgy enables "visualized assessment results, concrete improvement plans, and internationalized sustainability performance," substantively enhancing sustainable competitiveness.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What assessment dimensions does the Synesgy platform cover?
A: Synesgy questionnaires cover five dimensions: Management, Environment, Social, Governance, and Industry-Specific topics. The system also provides a simplified questionnaire for SMEs with fewer than 50 employees, enabling companies of all sizes to easily conduct self-assessments.

Q: What results do you receive after completing the assessment?
A: After completing the questionnaire, the platform instantly provides the company's ESG score and visual analytics reports. The system also awards Synesgy certificates aligned with international standards; companies can publish certificates on websites or social media to showcase their commitment and achievements on sustainability issues.

Q: Does Synesgy comply with international sustainability reporting standards?
A: Yes. Synesgy questionnaire design references international frameworks including GRI, the UN Global Compact, the Carbon Disclosure Project (CDP), and the EU Taxonomy. CRIF's questionnaire methodology, developed by CRIF Ratings, received official GRI certification in 2024, ensuring assessment content and results align with global best practices.
