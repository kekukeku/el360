One-stop ESG Self-Assessment Tool for Businesses

Synesgy is a global ESG self-assessment and certification platform developed by CRIF. It helps businesses evaluate their sustainability performance across governance, environmental, and social dimensions. By simply completing an online questionnaire covering key operational and supply chain aspects, companies receive real-time ESG scores, visual analytics, and a certificate aligned with international standards such as the SDGs, GRI, and ESRS. Certificates can be published on websites or social media to showcase a company’s sustainability commitment.

What is Synesgy?

Launched by CRIF, a global credit information and risk management company, Synesgy is a multilingual, multinational ESG assessment tool designed for companies of all sizes. It offers industry-specific questionnaires across five key sustainability areas: Governance, Environment, Social, Management, and Sector-specific topics. For SMEs with fewer than 50 employees, a simplified version is available. Synesgy helps companies perform ESG self-assessments, collect sustainability data from suppliers, and plan improvements based on international best practices.

Why Choose Synesgy?

Synesgy is backed by CRIF’s expertise in data and rating systems and follows globally recognized ESG frameworks including GRI, UN Global Compact, and the EU Taxonomy. In 2024, Synesgy became officially licensed by GRI, ensuring that its methodology aligns with top-tier international standards. The platform supports over 25 languages and is used in 60+ countries, making it ideal for multinational enterprises. With user-friendly dashboards and benchmarking tools, companies can easily monitor their own and their suppliers' ESG status while identifying operational or reputational risks. Once assessments are completed, companies receive Synesgy ESG certificates—ready for display on websites and social channels to build trust and credibility.

How It Works: Process & Deliverables

  1. Register & Input Data: Create a company account, fill in basic operational information, and upload relevant metrics.
  2. Answer the ESG Questionnaire: Respond to a set of questions customized to your company size and sector. SMEs receive a streamlined version.
  3. Receive Your Report & Certificate: Instantly access ESG scores, visual analytics, and an internationally aligned certificate. These documents offer clear insights into your strengths and areas for improvement. With Synesgy, managers can build data-driven strategies to enhance sustainability across their operations and supply chain.

Who Is It For?

Synesgy is designed for a broad range of users—from large corporations to small suppliers. For enterprises managing complex supply chains, Synesgy provides a clear picture of ESG performance across partners and vendors. Suppliers, in turn, benefit from actionable recommendations and visibility in sustainable sourcing programs. Financial institutions can use the platform to assess ESG risk in lending and investment portfolios, while trade associations can support member companies on their sustainability journeys. If your organization cares about ESG transparency and long-term growth, Synesgy is for you.

Results & Value

Synesgy is used in over 90 countries by hundreds of thousands of businesses and financial institutions. The platform delivers clear ESG scores and improvement plans aligned with global standards. The Synesgy certificate, recognized across industries, helps companies showcase their commitment to sustainability and social responsibility. Featuring comprehensive reports and visual benchmarks, Synesgy transforms ESG from a reporting obligation into a competitive advantage—supporting regulatory compliance, stakeholder communication, and market reputation.
